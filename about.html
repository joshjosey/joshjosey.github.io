Abroad

In the summer of 2023, I had the opportunity to study abroad at Texas Tech Costa Rica. This was truly one of the best experiences of my time as a student. During my 10 week program we had the opportunity to visit each province of the country and see some of the most well known places. One of the trips we went on was to the La Fortuna waterfall. After descending the 500 step stairway, getting to see the 250 foot waterfall and swim in the basin was an incredible experience. Although all of the excursions were incredible, the truly amazing part of this experince was the day to day living. Getting a glimpse into another culture and making my best efforts to put duolingo into practice made for an unforgettable journey.

Camp

In the summer of 2022 I worked as a counselor for Pine Cove Shores, spending around 2 months on Lake Palestine just outside of Tyler, Texas.
Growing up I was the furthest thing from a summer camp kid, but in my freshman year at Texas Tech, I believe God opened the door to work here to bring me closer to Him. Going by the camp name www.yoomama.com I led a cabin of around 8 young men each week. During their stay we went through a bible study called FRAME, focused on looking at yourself and others through the same frame as God. Working at Pine Cove was one of the times in my life where I grew the most as a believer and a leader.
